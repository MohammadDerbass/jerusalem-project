Grants

Our grants provide humanitarian assistance to Palestinians in the West Bank, Gaza and Jerusalem, and to
Palestinian refugees in Syria and Lebanon. We also support projects that help Palestinians highlight their
history and struggle to Western audiences

How do I order from the website?

To place an order, browse our products, add items to your cart, and proceed to checkout. A confirmation email will be sent once your order is placed.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept Visa, MasterCard, PayPal, and bank transfers. All transactions are processed securely.

Can I track my order status?

Yes. Once your order ships, you’ll receive a tracking number via email to monitor your package in real-time.

What is the return policy?

You can return products within 30 days of receipt. Items must be unused and in their original packaging. Contact our support team to begin the process.

Is there customer support available?

Yes. Our support team is available Monday through Friday, from 9 AM to 5 PM. Reach us via email or through the contact form on our website.

How long does shipping take?

Shipping usually takes 5–7 business days depending on your location. International shipping may take longer.

Can I cancel or change my order?

You can cancel or modify your order within 24 hours of placing it. After that, it may already be processed for shipment.

Development of Special Education Unit for Blind Children

This school has been serving the educational and therapy needs of blind children since 1982. Their latest project was the expansion of physio, occupational and speech therapy services for children enrolled in the school as well as those from neighboring areas.
