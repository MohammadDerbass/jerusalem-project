Location & Parking

The offices of The Jerusalem Fund and its educational program, the Palestine Center, are located on the ground level of the Potomac Plaza Building. The building is located near the Foggy Bottom Metro Station (orange and blue lines), on the Metrobus Line 80.

Limited parking is available on the street.

There is no parking available at the Potomac Plaza building. Only six spaces are available in front of The Jerusalem Fund offices and are shared with the building and a hair salon. To use them, please plan accordingly.
