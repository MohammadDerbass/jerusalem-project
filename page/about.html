Who we are

The Jerusalem Fund works to educate the United States’ public and policymakers about Palestine’s past, present and future, and provide community development support to communities in Palestine.

Empowering Communities,
Preserving Heritage

Since our founding in Washington, D.C. in 1991, The Jerusalem Fund has worked tirelessly to uplift Palestinian communities through education, cultural preservation, and sustainable development. Guided by principles of transparency, compassion, and solidarity, we partner with local leaders and institutions to deliver scholarships, healthcare programs, and emergency aid — touching hundreds of thousands of lives across the region.

Tarek Nasr

Finance and Operations Manager

Tarek Nasr is a graduate of American University’s School of International Service, where he majored in International Studies with a focus on the MENA region. Prior to working at The Jerusalem Fund, he worked with the Elephant in the Room Initiative, an organization of Jewish and Israeli professors speaking out against the Israeli occupation. He is excited to use his knowledge and past experiences to continue working for a brighter future in the region.
