MAR 15 FRI

Art & Culture: An Evening of Expression

Join us for a morning of stewardship along the banks of the Willow Creek! Volunteers of all ages will work together to remove litter, invasive plants, and debris, helping to restore habitat for fish and wildlife. We’ll provide gloves, trash bags, and a brief orientation on local species identification—just bring your enthusiasm and a reusable water bottle. Together, we can keep our waterways clean and healthy for generations to come.

Gallery 21 | 21 rue de la Paix 75002 Paris 6:00 PM - 9:00 PM Explore local art and engage with artists.
